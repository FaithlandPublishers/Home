Services

Assisted Self-Publishing

We specialize in assisted self-publishing packages, where we partner with you to bring your manuscript to life. Unlike traditional publishing, you retain control and responsibility for your work, while we provide expert guidance to ensure your book is market-ready.

Bespoke Publishing Packages

Our bespoke publishing packages offer a tailored approach to meet your specific needs. Whether you require additional book prints, unique interior designs, or specialized marketing support, we customize every aspect to ensure your book stands out.

Author Coaching

Our intensive coaching program is designed to equip authors with the knowledge and strategies needed to succeed. Through personalized sessions, we help you develop a business plan that maximizes your book's potential and opens new revenue streams.

Book Design and Printing

We offer comprehensive book design and printing services, ensuring that your book is visually captivating and professionally bound. From cover design to interior formatting, we handle every detail to create a product that will sell and change lives.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Faithland Publishers different from traditional publishers?

Faithland Publishers offers assisted self-publishing services, meaning you retain control over your work while receiving expert guidance. Unlike traditional publishers who assume the risks and control the go-to-market strategy, we empower you to take ownership of your publishing journey.

What support does Faithland Publishers provide throughout the publishing process?

We offer comprehensive support, including manuscript development, editing, design, and marketing. Our team is with you every step of the way, ensuring that your book is of the highest quality and ready for the market.

What is included in the bespoke publishing packages?

Our bespoke publishing packages are tailored to your needs and may include additional book prints, unique design features, and specialized marketing support. These packages are customizable to ensure your book receives the attention it deserves.

How does the coaching program benefit authors?

Our coaching program is designed to equip you with the tools and strategies needed to succeed as an author. Through personalized sessions, we help you develop a business plan that maximizes your book's impact and potential for generating revenue.

What should I expect during the publishing process?

The publishing process involves several stages, including manuscript submission, editing, design, and distribution. You will be actively involved at each stage, with our team providing guidance and support to ensure your book is a success.
